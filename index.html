Document Checklists

Required Documents by Visa Type

Every document category is critical. Missing even one item can lead to rejection. Below are the standard requirements — our team verifies your specific file before submission.

🇪🇺

Schengen Visa Documents

Required for all 27 Schengen zone countries (Germany, France, Italy, Spain, Netherlands, Austria, etc.)

⬇ Download Checklist PDF
🪪

Personal Documents

  • ✓
    Valid Passport — Original + all old passports. Must have min. 2 blank pages & be valid 3+ months beyond travel dates.
  • ✓
    Passport-Size Photos — 2 recent photos (35×45mm, white background, taken within last 6 months).
  • ✓
    National Identity Card (CNIC) — Photocopy of front and back.
  • ✓
    Visa Application Form — Completed, signed, and dated Schengen visa application form.
✈

Travel Documents

  • ✓
    Flight Itinerary / Round-Trip Booking — Confirmed or dummy round-trip flight reservation showing entry and exit dates.
  • ✓
    Hotel / Accommodation Proof — Hotel bookings, Airbnb confirmations, or host's invitation letter with their ID.
  • ✓
    Travel Insurance — Minimum coverage €30,000 for medical emergencies, valid for all Schengen countries and entire trip duration.
  • ✓
    Detailed Day-by-Day Itinerary — A clear travel plan for each day of your stay.
💰

Financial Documents

  • ✓
    Bank Statement (Last 6 Months) — Original, bank-stamped. Must show sufficient balance (min. €50–70 per day of stay recommended).
  • ✓
    Bank Certificate / Letter — On bank letterhead confirming account holder's name, account number, and available balance.
  • ✓
    Salary Slips / Income Proof — Last 3–6 months' salary slips from employer (if salaried).
  • ✓
    Tax Returns / FBR Documents — If self-employed or business owner, income tax returns for the last 1–2 years.
  • ✓
    Sponsor's Financial Documents — If someone else is sponsoring the trip, their bank statements, employment letter, and affidavit of support.
💼

Employment / Business Documents

  • ✓
    Employment Letter (NOC) — On company letterhead: position, salary, leave approval dates, and confirmation of return to job.
  • ✓
    Business Ownership Proof — NTN, business registration certificate, chamber membership (if self-employed).
  • ✓
    Leave Approval Letter — From employer confirming approved leave dates matching your travel period.
🏠

Ties to Home Country

  • ✓
    Property Documents — Property ownership papers showing you have assets in Pakistan and strong reason to return.
  • ✓
    Family Ties Evidence — Marriage certificate, children's birth certificates, or family registration certificate.
  • ✓
    Previous Travel History — Copies of all previous visas and entry/exit stamps from any country.
⚠️

Important: Requirements may vary slightly by embassy (German, French, Italian, etc.). Our team verifies your complete file against the specific consulate's current requirements before submission.

🇨🇦

Canada Visitor Visa (TRV) Documents

Temporary Resident Visa for tourism, family visits, or business travel to Canada.

⬇ Download Checklist PDF
🪪

Personal & Identity Documents

  • ✓
    Valid Passport — Must be valid for the entire duration of the planned stay in Canada.
  • ✓
    CNIC / Family Registration Certificate (FRC) — Original CNIC plus NADRA FRC for family applications.
  • ✓
    Passport-Size Photographs — Recent passport photos as per IRCC specifications.
  • ✓
    Birth Certificate — Required if applying with family members or for child applicants.
  • ✓
    Marriage Certificate — If including spouse in application (Nadra-issued or court-attested).
💰

Financial Documents

  • ✓
    Bank Statement (Last 6 Months) — Original, bank-stamped. Consistent balance demonstrating ability to fund the trip.
  • ✓
    Bank Certificate — Confirming relationship with bank, account type, and current balance.
  • ✓
    Salary Slips (Last 3–6 Months) — For salaried individuals as proof of regular income.
  • ✓
    Income Tax Returns (FBR) — Last 2 years' filed tax returns demonstrating financial standing.
  • ✓
    Property / Investment Portfolio — Proof of assets: property papers, shares, mutual funds, vehicle ownership, etc.
💼

Employment / Business Proof

  • ✓
    No Objection Certificate (NOC) — From employer on letterhead with job title, salary, leave approval, and guaranteed return to position.
  • ✓
    Business Registration Certificate — If self-employed: NTN, SECP registration, or chamber of commerce certificate.
  • ✓
    Business Bank Statement — Company's bank statement for business owners showing active operations.
🇨🇦

Canada Host / Invitation Documents

  • ✓
    Invitation Letter from Host in Canada — If visiting family/friends: their letter, Canadian PR/Citizenship, and proof of address.
  • ✓
    Host's Status in Canada — Copy of Canadian PR card, citizenship certificate, or passport.
  • ✓
    Accommodation Proof — Hotel bookings or host's address proof if staying with family/friends.
✈

Travel & Purpose Documents

  • ✓
    Flight Itinerary — Booked or dummy round-trip reservation clearly showing arrival/departure dates.
  • ✓
    Travel Itinerary / Purpose Letter — Detailed letter explaining purpose of visit, planned activities, and commitment to return.
  • ✓
    Previous Travel History — Copies of all prior visas (especially USA, UK, Schengen) significantly strengthen Canada TRV applications.
💡

Pro Tip: Canada places strong emphasis on ties to home country and financial stability. A consistent 6-month bank history with no sudden large deposits is critical. Our team helps you build the strongest possible financial narrative.

🇦🇺

Australia Visitor Visa (Subclass 600) Documents

For tourism, family visits, or business travel to Australia. Applied online via ImmiAccount.

⬇ Download Checklist PDF
🪪

Identity & Personal Documents

  • ✓
    Valid Passport (Scanned Copy) — Bio-data page. Passport must be valid for the duration of your stay.
  • ✓
    CNIC (Scanned) — Front and back of national identity card.
  • ✓
    Digital Passport Photo — Recent photo meeting Australian immigration specifications.
  • ✓
    Marriage / Birth Certificates — If applying with family members (must be translated to English if in Urdu).
💰

Financial Evidence

  • ✓
    Bank Statements (Last 6 Months) — Showing sufficient funds to cover the entire trip. Australia expects clear, consistent financial history.
  • ✓
    Bank Letter / Certificate — Confirming account details and current balance.
  • ✓
    Salary Slips / Proof of Income — Last 3 months' payslips or business income evidence.
  • ✓
    Tax Returns (FBR) — Demonstrates established financial presence in Pakistan.
  • ✓
    Assets Proof — Property papers, vehicle ownership, investments showing you have reasons to return.
💼

Employment / Occupation

  • ✓
    Employment Letter — On company letterhead: designation, salary, leave approval, and confirmation you will return to your position.
  • ✓
    Business Evidence — If self-employed: NTN, business registration, bank statement showing business activity.
✈

Travel Purpose & Plans

  • ✓
    Travel Itinerary / Purpose Statement — Clear explanation of your visit purpose, planned activities, and accommodation details.
  • ✓
    Flight Bookings (Return) — Dummy or confirmed round-trip flight reservations.
  • ✓
    Hotel / Accommodation Confirmation — Bookings or invitation letter from Australian host with their status documents.
  • ✓
    Previous International Travel — Copies of all prior visas especially USA, UK, Schengen. Significantly strengthens Australian applications.
🏥

Additional Requirements

  • ✓
    Health Insurance — Proof of adequate health insurance for the duration of your stay in Australia.
  • ✓
    Character Declaration — You may be asked to provide a police clearance certificate (PCC) from Pakistan.
⚠️

Note: Australia's visitor visa is entirely online via ImmiAccount. There is no embassy appointment. Processing typically takes 15–60 days. Our team manages the complete online application on your behalf.

🇬🇧

UK Standard Visitor Visa Documents

For tourism, family visits, medical treatment, or short business trips to the United Kingdom.

⬇ Download Checklist PDF
🪪

Personal Documents

  • ✓
    Valid Passport — All old passports must also be submitted. Passport must cover entire intended stay.
  • ✓
    CNIC — Photocopy of both sides of national identity card.
  • ✓
    Passport-Size Photos — As per UKVI specifications (45mm × 35mm, light grey or cream background).
  • ✓
    Family Documents — Marriage certificate and children's birth certificates (if applicable).
💰

Financial Documents

  • ✓
    Bank Statement (Last 6 Months) — Original stamped. UK Home Office scrutinizes transactions carefully — avoid large unexplained deposits.
  • ✓
    Bank Certificate — Official letter from bank on letterhead confirming account status and balance.
  • ✓
    Salary Slips (Last 6 Months) — For salaried employees to demonstrate consistent income.
  • ✓
    Income Tax Returns — FBR tax returns for the past 2 years (especially important for self-employed applicants).
  • ✓
    Property / Asset Evidence — Ownership documents showing financial roots in Pakistan.
💼

Employment / Business

  • ✓
    Employer's NOC / Leave Letter — Confirming role, salary, leave approval, and guaranteed return to employment.
  • ✓
    Business Registration / NTN — For business owners: full documentation of business ownership and activity.
  • ✓
    Company Financial Statement — Business bank statements and accounts showing viable business operations.
✈

Travel Plans & Purpose

  • ✓
    Cover Letter — Detailed personal letter explaining purpose, itinerary, accommodation plan, and clear intent to return.
  • ✓
    Flight Itinerary (Return) — Round-trip flight booking confirming travel dates.
  • ✓
    Accommodation Proof — Hotel reservations or UK host's address proof and invitation letter.
  • ✓
    UK Contact / Sponsor Letter — If visiting family or friends: their UK status documents, address proof, and support letter.
  • ✓
    Prior Visa History — Copies of Schengen, US, Canadian, or other prior visas. Excellent travel history greatly strengthens UK applications.
💡

UK Tip: The UK Home Office places exceptional weight on your cover letter and the "genuine visitor" test. Our team crafts precise, compelling cover letters tailored to your specific circumstances and travel history.

🎓

European Student Visa Documents

General requirements for student visas to Germany, France, Austria, Italy, Poland & other European countries.

⬇ Download Checklist PDF
🪪

Personal Identity Documents

  • ✓
    Valid Passport — Must be valid for at least 6 months beyond the visa expiry date. All old passports to be included.
  • ✓
    CNIC / NICOP — Pakistani national identity card or NICOP for overseas Pakistanis.
  • ✓
    Passport-Size Photos — Recent biometric photos meeting the specific embassy's specifications.
  • ✓
    Birth Certificate — Nadra-issued or equivalent, may need to be attested / apostilled.
🏫

Academic & Admission Documents

  • ✓
    University Admission Letter — Official letter of admission from the European university confirming your enrolled program.
  • ✓
    Academic Transcripts & Certificates — Attested copies of your degree(s), HEC-attested transcripts and certificates.
  • ✓
    APS Certificate (Germany Only) — Academic Evaluation Centre certificate verifying Pakistani academic qualifications for German universities.
  • ✓
    Language Certificate — IELTS, TestDaF, DELF, or equivalent language proficiency test results as required by your university.
  • ✓
    Statement of Purpose (SOP) — A strong, university-specific personal statement explaining your motivation and study plans.
💰

Financial Proof of Subsistence

  • ✓
    Blocked Account (Germany) — €11,208 minimum deposited in a German blocked account (Sperrkonto) via Fintiba, Coracle, or Deutsche Bank.
  • ✓
    Scholarship Letter — Official award letter if you have a DAAD, Eiffel, Erasmus+, or institutional scholarship.
  • ✓
    Sponsor's Financial Documents — If family-sponsored: bank statements, CNIC, salary slips, property papers, and formal sponsorship declaration (Verpflichtungserklärung for Germany).
  • ✓
    Bank Statement (Self-funded) — Applicant's or sponsor's recent 6-month bank statement showing adequate funds.
🏠

Accommodation & Insurance

  • ✓
    Proof of Accommodation in Europe — Confirmation of student dormitory, private rental agreement, or host's address confirmation.
  • ✓
    Health / Travel Insurance — Comprehensive health insurance valid in the destination country for at least the first 90 days.
✈

Travel Documents

  • ✓
    Flight Booking / Itinerary — One-way or return flight booking showing planned arrival date in the destination country.
  • ✓
    Visa Application Form — Completed and signed national visa (long-stay) application form for the specific country.
🇩🇪

Germany Note: Germany requires the APS certificate before applying for a student visa — this process takes 6–10 weeks. Start early. Our team coordinates the entire timeline including APS, blocked account, TestDaF registration, and visa appointment simultaneously.
